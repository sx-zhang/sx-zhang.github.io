Biography

Sixian Zhang received his Ph.D. degree from the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), in 2025, under the supervision of Shuqiang Jiang (蒋树强) (Professor at the UCAS), and Xinhang Song (宋新航) (Associate Professor at ICT, CAS). Since July 2025, he has been an Assistant Researcher (Post-doc Fellow) at the State Key Laboratory of AI Safety, ICT, CAS. His research interests include embodied AI and visual navigation. He has received several awards, including the CAS President Award, the Excellent Graduate of Beijing Award, and the Postdoctoral Innovation Talent Support Program.

张思贤，中国科学院计算技术研究所特别研究助理（合作导师：蒋树强研究员）。 长期从事具身智能和视觉导航等方向的研究， 在CCF/CAAI推荐A类会议/期刊发表论文14篇， 其中以第一作者发表论文9篇（包括IEEE TPAMI、CVPR、ICCV、ECCV） 。 获2025博士后创新人才支持计划支持，中科院院长优秀奖，北京市优秀毕业生等。 学术服务方面，担任CVPR、ICCV、NeurIPS、ICLR、ICML等CCF-A类会议审稿人。
